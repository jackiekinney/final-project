Blend a Great Smoothie

Smoothies are great because they make it easy to add fresh fruit and vegetables to your diet. They can als be taken on the go and don't require a lot of cooking.

Tips to Save Time and Money

  • Get triple-washed greens

    No need to wash them ahead of time!

  • Get clamshells, not plastic bags

    Your greens will last longer.

  • Prepare raw ingredients in batches

    Bought a whole pineapple or a bunch of greens? Wash and cut it all in one go then store in a zip-lock bag or tupperware for easy use.

  • Try frozen fruit

    It's often more affordable, requires no prep, and it lasts longer. It also makes your smoothie taste like a frozen dessert!
